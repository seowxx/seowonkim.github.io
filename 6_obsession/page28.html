Plot

A young girl who belongs to Father Kim's parish becomes comatose after a hit-and-run accident. Father Kim finds out she is possessed by an evil spirit. Father Kim along with a rebellious young seminarian named Choi who is unsure about the spiritual warfare, tries to exorcise the demon and confine it in a piglet. Rosicrucianism informs Father Kim two of their priests was killed by this demon. Manifestations of the demon terrifies Choi and he runs out, but comes back to finish what they started with the determination. Posters

The Korean version of the poster features an image of two main priests of the film. The text in the center of the poster reads, "Seoul, 2015, the chosen became alive." It was a box office hit in Korea, and most audience knew the movie was about exorcism even before the movie premiered. Maybe it became famous too early, but there is no mention of exorcism in the Korean poster. The U.S. poster has a disturbing image of a child getting exorcism by one of the priest. A cross is pushed on the posessed child's forehead, and the child is in pain. The U.S. poster is better at gaining audience's attention becuase of the image. The audience can expect a horror film with priests and posessed one by looking at the U.S. poster.
